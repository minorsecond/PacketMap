Packet Radio Map

Nodes & digipeaters in the Denver area are scanned every 15 minutes.

Every half hour, a random NET/ROM node is selected. This node is checked for new nodes, and their MHeard list is scanned for new operators & digipeaters.

If you have a suggestion for a new node or digipeater, click the light bulb icon. The entry won't be added to the map immediately, but will be added the next time the call shows up on an MHeard list.
